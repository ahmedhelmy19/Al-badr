About Us

Our History

Al Badr Company was established in 2012 as a joint venture company specialized in suspended ceilings and finishing at a time when the field was in its early stages and helped create a huge boom. The population's need for the rapidly increasing demand for the field had to be met through housing, commercial and educational facilities, medical centers and hospitals. Traditional construction methods had become slow, labor-intensive and impractical in many cases. It was time to look to the future of construction through fast-implementing suspended ceilings.
